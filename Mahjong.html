How to play Mahjong Solitaire

Match pairs of identical free tiles to remove them. A tile is free when nothing rests on top of it and either its left or right side is unblocked. Clear all 72 tiles to win.

Strategy

Plan ahead — bad early matches can lock the board. When you see four of a kind exposed, match all four; otherwise, match pairs that unblock the most tiles. Use HINT when stuck, or SHUFFLE if no moves remain.
