What this site does

This site hosts free in-browser games. The games run entirely on your device. Game state (scores, settings, save slots) is stored in your browser's local storage and never leaves your device.

Advertising

The ad-supported version of this site uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google and its third-party vendors use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites.

  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to this site and/or other sites on the Internet.
  • You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.
  • You may opt out of third-party vendor use of cookies for personalized advertising via aboutads.info or youronlinechoices.com.

The clean version of this site (index.html) does not display advertisements. It does load the Google AdSense library script for ownership verification, but no ad slots render on that page.

Analytics

This site does not use first-party analytics. AdSense may collect aggregate impression and click data for ad delivery — see Google's privacy policy for details.

Data storage on your device

Individual games may store progress in your browser's localStorage. This data stays on your device. Clearing your browser storage removes it. None of it is transmitted to any server.

Children

This site is intended for general audiences. Per AdSense policy, personalized advertising is not served to users known to be under the age of 13.
