What this site does

This site hosts free in-browser games. The games run entirely on your device. Game state (scores, settings, save slots) is stored in your browser's local storage and never leaves your device.

Advertising

This site is supported by Google AdSense. Google and its third-party vendors use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites.

  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to this site and/or other sites on the Internet.
  • You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.
  • You may opt out of third-party vendor use of cookies for personalized advertising via aboutads.info or youronlinechoices.com.
  • EEA, UK, and Switzerland visitors see a consent banner managed by Google's certified CMP before personalized ads are served.

Analytics

This site does not use first-party analytics. AdSense may collect aggregate impression and click data for ad delivery — see Google's privacy policy for details.

Data storage on your device

Individual games may store progress in your browser's localStorage. This data stays on your device. Clearing your browser storage removes it. None of it is transmitted to any server.

Android apps

The same games are also published as standalone Android apps on the Google Play Store. Apps in this collection may include, but are not limited to:

Cliffwalkers · Floodline · Tower (SkyStack) · Bonneville Spillway Operator · Apoapsis · Biosphere Blue · Doctrine · Agora · Aresia · Backgammon · Bisque · Chess · Convergence · Go · Mancala · Odyssey · Othello · Pente Grammai · Senet · Tidelands · Ur

The Android apps:

  • Run entirely on your device. Game progress is stored only in the app's local storage and never transmitted to any server we control.
  • Do not collect personally identifiable information (no accounts, no logins, no email collection).
  • Do not require an internet connection to play (after install).

The Android apps display advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and process the following information for the purpose of serving relevant ads:

  • Your device's advertising identifier — a resettable ID separate from any account.
  • General device information (model, OS version, language, country).
  • Approximate location derived from IP address.
  • Whether you interact with an ad.

You can reset your advertising identifier or limit ad tracking at any time from your device's Settings > Privacy > Ads. For details on how Google handles this data, see Google's privacy policy and the AdMob privacy disclosures.

Children

This site and these Android apps are intended for general audiences. Per Google's policies, personalized advertising is not served to users known to be under the age of 13. The Android apps are rated Everyone on Google Play and target users aged 13 and older.
