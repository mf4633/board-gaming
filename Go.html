Rules

  • Black plays first. Place stones on intersections of the grid lines.
  • Stones of the same color connected horizontally/vertically form a group.
  • A group's liberties are the number of empty adjacent intersections.
  • When a group has zero liberties, it is captured and removed from the board.
  • You may not play a stone that would have no liberties (suicide), unless it captures opponent stones.
  • Ko rule: you may not recreate the immediately previous board position.
  • Either player may pass. Two consecutive passes end the game.
  • Scoring (Chinese/area rules): stones on the board + surrounded empty territory.
