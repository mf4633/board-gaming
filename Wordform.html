How to play Wordform

Guess the hidden 5-letter word in six tries. After each guess, the tiles flip:

right letter, right spot  ·  right letter, wrong spot  ·  not in the word

One puzzle per day on DAILY mode (resets at midnight local). Use RANDOM for unlimited play, or SHARE to copy your daily result as an emoji grid.
