Rules

  • Each player has 7 pieces. Race them along the path from start to finish.
  • Roll 4 binary dice (total 0-4). Move one piece forward by that amount.
  • White travels down the left column, across the shared middle, and back up the left. Black mirrors on the right.
  • Landing on an opponent on the shared middle lane captures them (sent back to start).
  • Flower squares (rosettes) grant an extra roll and are safe from capture.
  • You must roll the exact number needed to bear a piece off the board.
  • First player to bear off all 7 pieces wins!
