AGORA

The Mediterranean Trade

Rules

  • Two merchants compete to accumulate 30 coins through trade on the Mediterranean.
  • Roll phase: Roll 2d6. Hexes matching the total produce their resource for all adjacent trade posts.
  • A roll of 7 triggers a market crash — the most expensive resource drops 3 price levels.
  • Trade phase: Buy and sell resources at the Agora (center market). Prices move with supply and demand.
  • Build phase: Build trade posts at cities (1 Grain + 1 Marble) or trade routes between cities (1 Oil + 1 Silk).
  • You must have a trade route connected to the Agora (center) to buy or sell.
  • Click a city on the map to build a trade post there. Click a route (edge) to build a trade route.
  • First to 30 coins wins! Trade posts and routes count as 2 coins each at game end.
